    Azure Cloud + Azure DevOps — A Complete Beginner's Guide

    LearnWithMithran · YouTube · 29 Days · Zero assumed cloud knowledge

    What this course covers

    Starting from absolute zero, this course takes you through every major area of Azure — from spinning up your first virtual machine to deploying a full application with Azure DevOps pipelines. Every concept is taught through the Azure Portal (GUI-first), and every demo step is clearly labeled so you know exactly what's free and what costs money.

    Who this is for

    Complete beginners to cloud computing. No prior experience with any cloud platform (Azure, AWS, GCP) or on-premise infrastructure is assumed. If you can open a browser, you can follow this course.

    How to follow along

    Instructor account: Paid Azure — used to demonstrate all features.

    Your account: Azure Free Tier — sufficient for 95%+ of this course.

    Every portal demo step is labeled:

    Label Meaning
    ✅ Free Tier You can do this step on a free account
    💳 Paid — Instructor Demo Watch along; skip or activate a trial to practice
