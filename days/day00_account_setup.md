# Day 0 — Azure Account Setup & Free Tier Walkthrough

> Phase 0 — Foundations + Account Setup

## Goal

Get every viewer set up with a free Azure account, understand the free tier, and feel confident navigating the portal before spending anything.

## Key Topics

- What is Azure? Microsoft's global cloud computing platform
- Creating a free Azure account at portal.azure.com
  - $200 free credit for 30 days
  - 12 months of free services after credit expires
  - Always-free services (25+ services that never expire)
- Credit card requirement — reassurance: card is for identity verification only; you will not be charged without explicitly upgrading
- Azure for Students option (no credit card needed — if eligible)
- Setting up Budget Alerts to avoid surprise bills
- Tour of the Azure Portal: home screen, search bar, resource groups, subscriptions
- Azure regions — what they are and how to pick one closest to you

## Hands-On Demo

**Account Requirements:** All steps are free tier — students can follow every step.

- ✅ Create an Azure free account live at portal.azure.com
- ✅ Set a $1 budget alert (Cost Management → Budgets)
- ✅ Navigate the portal: find Subscriptions, Resource Groups, Cost Management

## Summary

By the end of this video, every viewer has a working Azure free account, a safety budget alert in place, and knows their way around the portal. No money spent, no surprises.
